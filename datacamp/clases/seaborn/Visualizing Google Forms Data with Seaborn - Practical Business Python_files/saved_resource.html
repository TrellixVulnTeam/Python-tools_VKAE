        Thanks so much for the tutorial. Everything has worked great until I get to the factorplot. I ended up with "TypeError: unsupported operand type(s) for /: 'str' and 'long'".

        I just found the solution though. In seaborn 0.6, you need to specify kind='count' within factorplot.

        Using something like

        sns.factorplot("os", data=results, kind = "count", palette="BuPu")

        allowed me to get factorplot to work.

        More info at https://github.com/mwaskom/...

        Thanks again for your awesome tutorial.
